LIVING AND WORKING IN LOS ALAMOS

Beyond the science, CTA members live in one of the most striking corners of the country: a small mountain town on the Pajarito Plateau, surrounded by national forest, dark skies, and the high desert of Northern New Mexico.

THE LABORATORY

Summary

Los Alamos National Laboratory is one of the largest employers in New Mexico, with roughly 13,000 staff — including about 2,500 research and development staff, 500 postdocs, and 1,600 students.

The Laboratory sits in beautiful Northern New Mexico, about a 40-minute drive northwest of Santa Fe, with outstanding opportunities for the outdoor enthusiast right outside the door.
