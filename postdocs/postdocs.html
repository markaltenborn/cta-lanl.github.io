CTA POSTDOCS

The Center for Theoretical Astrophysics (CTA) hosts a vibrant postdoctoral program in theoretical and computational astrophysics at Los Alamos National Laboratory. Our postdocs work closely with staff and students on cutting-edge research and on projects that connect astrophysics to broader LANL missions.

What you'll work on

  • Numerical simulations and modeling in astrophysics
  • High-performance computing and multi-physics codes
  • Connecting theory with observations and survey data

Who we're looking for

  • PhD in astronomy, astrophysics, physics, or related field
  • Strong background in computation, theory, or data analysis
  • Interest in collaborative, cross-disciplinary research

Why LANL & CTA

  • World-class supercomputing and research environment
  • Structured LANL postdoc program and mentoring
  • Competitive salary and generous benefits
