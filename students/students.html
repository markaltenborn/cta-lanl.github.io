CTA STUDENTS

The Center for Theoretical Astrophysics (CTA) hosts undergraduate and graduate students through Los Alamos National Laboratory's student programs. Each year, students join us to work on research projects in theoretical and computational astrophysics, gaining experience in a national laboratory environment while advancing their academic goals.

Student opportunities

  • Summer and year-round research appointments
  • Work with CTA mentors on defined research projects
  • Opportunities to present and publish your work

Who we're looking for

  • Undergraduate and graduate students in physics, astronomy, applied math, computer science, or related fields
  • Strong interest in astrophysics and scientific computing
  • Curiosity, initiative, and willingness to learn new tools

Why LANL & CTA

  • Be part of a large, lab-wide student program (1,800+ interns annually)
  • Access to LANL's high-performance computing resources
  • Mentoring, seminars, and networking across the Laboratory
